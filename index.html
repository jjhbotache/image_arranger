Welcome to Image Arranger

Image Arranger allows you to visually organize images in a gallery. Here's a brief guide to help you get started:

  • Image Gallery: On the right, visualize your images organized in rows and columns.
  • Column Controller: Adjust the number of columns to customize the arrangement of your images.
  • Add Images: Use the "Add image" button to add new images to the gallery.
  • Remove Images: Use the "Remove image" button to remove images from the gallery.
  • Photo Theme: Enter the desired theme to label your images.

Explore and experiment to create your own image arrangement!
